Alice?

  • Genre: Adventure, Puzzle
  • Language: C++ & Unreal Engine
  • Target Platform: PC
  • Timeframe: October 2014 - April 2015
  • Team: This was a solo project created for the final year of my college course.
  • Source Code

What would you do if you were trapped in a dream? If the world had turned against you, and escape was no where in sight? An amnesiac given the name "Alice" seems to have found herself in such a predicament. The only thing she is certain of is her ability to take the shapes of other creatures and make use of their skill sets.
