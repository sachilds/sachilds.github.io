Updated the Red Chillie website from it's pure image-based website/menu to an easier to update version for management. This was done to prevent customer's from receiving inaccurate information from the online menu and provide an easier navigation system. The base of the design was the pre-existing paper menus available in the restaurant, providing the colour scheme, images and primary fonts.
